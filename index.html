Your Future is Greater than Drugs

Why Avoid Drugs?

  • Drug abuse damages the brain and weakens memory.
  • It leads to poor health and shortens life expectancy.
  • It causes family and relationship breakdowns.
  • It increases crime and violence in communities.
  • It steals your dreams and opportunities for the future.

Healthy Alternatives

  • Sports & Exercise
  • Learning Coding
  • Playing Music
  • Reading & Education
  • Volunteering
